Become An Agent

JOIN OUR TEAM OF GREATNESS

At CAM-GD Homes, we consider our realtors as integral members of our team, bringing a culture of support and collaboration.

Your obligations as an Agent

Help clients get the best possible price for value from home sales.

Market properties on multiple platforms to help them sell fast.

Handle negotiations between you and the buyer to ensure favorable outcomes.

Perks & Benefits

Access to relevant resources in real estate.
Enjoy flexible commission plans that grow with you.
Marketing support for your listings and leads.
Regular training and growth opportunities.

Terms and Conditions

You shall be entitled to a 10–13% commission on any sales (excluding the interest from sales).

Mark-ups of any kind without the company’s consent are strictly prohibited.

A photocopy of a valid ID (voter’s card, driver’s license, or national identity card) is required.

Additional Incentives

Upon registration, you’ll gain access to all marketing materials.

Monthly awards for top-performing agents.

Car bonuses and gift rewards valued up to ₦1.5 million from high sales.
